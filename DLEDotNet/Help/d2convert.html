Level Converter

Levels can be converted into different formats, as well as exported into Overload levels (only geometry).

When converting a Descent 1 level to a Descent 2 (including Vertigo and D2X-XL) level, a separate texture conversion dialog will appear that allows you to customize how the textures will be converted. The texture on the left shows the texture to be converted, while the texture on the right will show the texture that it will be converted to. Make sure to go through the list on the left.
