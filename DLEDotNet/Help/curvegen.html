Curve Generator

The curve generator can be used to automatically generate curved tunnels between two sides. To use the curve generator, start by selecting the sides, one as the current side and the other as the other side. It is accessed from Tools > Curve Generator or by pressing G.

The generated tunnel will follow a BÃ©zier curve, the parameters of which can be adjusted by using Ctrl+8 and Ctrl+9 to control the curvature (by the distance of the control points from the sides; 8 increases and 9 decreases) and Alt+8 and Alt+9 to adjust the granularity (length of segments in the tunnel; 8 makes segments shorter and 9 makes them longer). All of these operations will be done for the current side, but you can use Space to switch to the other segment so that the same operations can be done on the other side.

The tunnel can be twisted by changing the current point by pressing P.

The tunnel will adopt the textures of the current segment. To finish creating the tunnel, click Tools > Curve Generator again or press G to confirm whether the tunnel should be created or not.
